About Me

A passionate learner at the crossroads of technology and human-centered design.

My Journey

Hello! I'm Anup Paudel, a Computer Science student at Community College of Baltimore County (CCBC) Essex, currently in my second semester after transferring in Fall 2025. Originally from Nepal, I came to the United States to pursue my computer science degree and have been passionate about exploring the intersection of technology, design, and human experience.

I started my journey at Webster University in Saint Louis, Missouri, where I completed two semesters focusing on programming fundamentals, operating systems, and problem-solving. I was honored to be on the Dean's List in Fall 2024. My academic path has been driven by curiosity—from competitive programming (BYTE_B@SH competition) to exploring various domains including AI, data science, and web development.

My passion extends beyond traditional coding. I'm equally excited about frontend design, UI/UX design, video editing, and graphic design. This multidisciplinary approach helps me create solutions that are not only functional but also visually compelling and user-centered. I'm currently deepening my knowledge in data analytics and Python, with aspirations to specialize in data science. I leverage AI tools daily—Gemini, NotebookLM for learning, and ChatGPT for problem-solving—to accelerate my growth and explore innovative approaches.
